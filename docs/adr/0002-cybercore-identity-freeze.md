# ADR 0002: CyberCore Identity Freeze v1.0

Date: 2026-07-15

Status: Accepted

## Context

CyberCore needed a stable identity, decision model, design direction, terminology and governance before implementation accelerated.

The identity was reviewed jointly by Jan and Eimy in a structured workshop.

## Decision

Adopt the following as CyberCore Identity v1.0:

- Primary descriptor: Infrastructure Context & Intelligence Platform
- Technical descriptor: AI-first Infrastructure Control Plane
- Philosophy: CyberCore exists to reduce uncertainty by turning operational knowledge into reusable intelligence.
- Vision: CyberCore should give engineers back the time they deserve to spend on what matters most.
- Human principle: Technology serves people. Never the other way around.
- Motto: Build knowledge. Earn confidence. Enable automation.
- Open-source model: Open Framework. Private Overlay.
- Design direction: Minimalist Cyberpunk Engineering.

## Consequences

- Future architecture and product decisions are evaluated against this identity.
- Constitutional changes require unanimous approval from Jan and Eimy.
- Public framework logic must remain separate from private operational knowledge.
- Automation follows Evidence, Knowledge and Confidence rather than preceding them.

## Review trigger

Revisit only when the current identity materially blocks project purpose, integrity or long-term sustainability.
