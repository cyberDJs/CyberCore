# CyberCore Identity v1.0

Status: Accepted

## Name

CyberCore

## Primary descriptor

> Infrastructure Context & Intelligence Platform

## Technical descriptor

> AI-first Infrastructure Control Plane

Technical descriptor only.

## Philosophy

> CyberCore exists to reduce uncertainty by turning operational knowledge into reusable intelligence.

## Vision

> CyberCore should give engineers back the time they deserve to spend on what matters most.

## Human principle

> Technology serves people. Never the other way around.

## Engineering motto

> Build knowledge. Earn confidence. Enable automation.

## Engineering principles

1. Know what exists.
2. Understand why it's there.
3. Keep only what creates value.
4. Automate only what is understood.
5. Complexity is guilty until proven useful.

## Decision model

```text
Reality
  -> Evidence
  -> Knowledge
  -> Confidence
  -> Decision
  -> Automation
```

## Open-source strategy

> Open Framework. Private Overlay.

## Design philosophy

> Minimalist Cyberpunk Engineering

Mission Control clarity with hackerspace character. Visual style must never reduce readability.

## North-star question

> Does this reduce uncertainty or return meaningful time to engineers?

If the answer is no, the feature probably does not belong in CyberCore.
