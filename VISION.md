# CyberCore Vision

CyberCore is intended to become an open-source-ready framework for operating a small but serious digital infrastructure ecosystem with AI-assisted operations.

The project starts as the operational backbone for Eimy Herrer / CyberDJS, but is designed so the private parts can be separated from reusable framework logic.

## Long-term idea

A lightweight, modular control plane for:

- personal and creative infrastructure
- small business infrastructure
- artist ecosystems
- self-hosted tools
- documentation automation
- observability
- low-cost DevSecOps
- knowledge graph operations

## Non-goals

- Do not become a bloated enterprise platform.
- Do not require Kubernetes unless justified.
- Do not require expensive SaaS.
- Do not store secrets in Git.
- Do not automate destructive actions without explicit approval.
